About Us

Welcome to the official site of the "Primetransport" forwarding company.

Primetransport forwarding company is a part of PRIMEGROUP companies (among which Primesurvey and Zerno-Port companies). We can arrange a delivery of different export/import and transit cargoes shipped by sea, river, air, railway and road transport.

Thanks to many years of practical experience of working in the forwarding market we provide a high-quality worldwide forwarding service of all types of general cargoes (including sensitive, dangerous, out-of-gauge and even military cargoes), bulk and liquid cargoes, as well as containerized cargoes.

The main priority of our company is the complete satisfaction of customers needs and expectations with the help of our maximum attention to the transportation process, round-the-clock information about it and involving all resources for safe and timely cargo delivery. Our strong point is the ability to organize a full range of transport services including preparation of all types of documents, such as: bill of lading, CMR, import/export/transit declarations, special permits and certificates, etc.

What We Offer

Sea Freight

Sea transport is the most versatile and profitable type of transportation. Therefore, more than 80% of goods are transported by this mode of transport. Shipping of all types of general, liquid and bulk cargoes using GENERAL/MPP/RORO/BULK vessels from 1'000 mt and up to vessels of the “PANAMAX” type.

Air Freight

Air cargo delivery is a convenient and the fastest form of shipping. The types of shipments usually delivered by air are high-value goods, medical supplies, perishable products and time critical cargo. Your shipments will reach their destination quickly, safely, and in perfect condition.

Railway Freight

We offer railway shipping in any direction within the Eurasian continent. The types of shipments usually delivered by rail are out-of-gauge cargo, building materials, coal, wood, metal, cement, limestone, petroleum products, grains.

Road Freight

Road transportation from and to Ukraine, around Europe. We can find small, medium and heavy trucks for our client’s needs, as well as special equipment for out-of-gauge cargo. Our company can offer freight shipping by curtain trucks, refrigerators, low loaders, tanks, car transporters, etc.

Multimodal Transport

In the absence of direct transport connection between a shipper and a consignee we can provide a service of multimodal transport, which means transportation of goods under a single contract, but performed with several modes of transport. The cargo can be delivered from port directly to the consignee's warehouse by using railway or road transport.

Groupage Transport

We offer LCL (Less-than-Container-load) and LTL(Less-than-Truckload) services for sea and road freight. Consolidation makes shipping of small consignments much more cost effective. We also provide warehousing services in Europe, Asia and North America for consolidation or arranging multimodal deliveries.

Cargo Insurance

Cargo insurance is an important option for transportation. It helps to reduce financial risk, related to unforeseen circumstances and force majeure. Primetransport company will help to insure any type of cargo for air, road, sea and railway international transportation.



For ensuring non-stop transportation and full set of service we arrange to our clients the following:
  • full spectrum of forwarding service for all types of cargoes
  • customs clearance and certification of cargoes
  • full logistical service for import/export and transit cargoes
  • cargo survey. Partner company PRIMESURVEY
  • insurance of cargoes and shipments
  • booking, stuffing/unstaffing of containers
  • organisation of multimodal transportations: truck/rail - vessel, truck - aircraft cargo movement tracing
We always follow the changes in our field and keep up with the technological developments in order to be more successful in our business.
